Chapter 1: Not Yet

The timing isn't right, the stars haven't aligned,
A beautiful connection, left trapped inside the mind.
We dance around the edges of what we want to say,
Holding back the words, keeping feelings at bay.

Not yet time to open up the locked internal door,
Though every quiet instinct keeps pulling back for more.
So we wait within the margins, content to let it grow,
Nurturing a steady flame that only we two know.
