I'll Follow You Anywhere - Villa Nero (Ch. 9-27)

The gates of Villa Nero loomed ahead, dark iron structures twisting up toward a starlit canopy.
It was a fortress hidden deep within the high cliffs, away from the frantic noise of the coastal trade routes.

"We stay here until the alignment is complete," he whispered, looking up at the high stone parapets.
She nodded, stepping through the entryway. The architecture inside was ancient, built on complex geometries that felt entirely isolated from the rest of the world—a perfect sanctuary for the stories left to tell.
