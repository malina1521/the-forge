The Gallery At Josie's (Mel's Version)

The lighting in the gallery was designed to create illusions, casting long, dramatic shadows across the polished concrete floors.
I stood near the center frame, pretending to analyze the abstract textures, but my attention was entirely anchored to the footsteps approaching from behind.

"It lacks symmetry," a voice noted quietly, matching my exact pacing.
I turned slightly, catching the deliberate alignment in his expression. "Symmetry is an artificial constraint. The true narrative lives in the spaces where the lines intentionally fracture."
