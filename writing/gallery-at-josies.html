The Gallery at Josie's

BY M

You open the door as we walk into Josie’s Bistro a few minutes before six. You take my hand as we approach the maître d’ podium near the entrance. I speak up before you can. “Reservation under Picard.” From the corner of my eye, I watch you perk up, surprised with a cheeky grin, your eyes expressive of such beautiful love. You're beaming as if I've made a grand gesture. I love how you get excited about the little things.

I bite back a chuckle as I think to myself, If only you knew. I am happy you're already happy, but also full of nervous excitement because the real gesture is yet to come.

I stare out at the dining room, recognizing the same white table settings that contrast perfectly with the dark furniture and decor. The spotlights around the room make my eyes immediately travel to the walls. The gallery dining room is what Josie’s is known for across the state.

The gentleman at the podium scans the list, then he looks up with a knowing smile. “It will be just a few minutes.”

You nod, and we step aside in the lobby. I'm doing my best not to fidget while we wait for our table, but the excitement is getting the best of me. I know you've caught on.

You tuck a loose curl behind my ear as you watch me with keen suspicion. “Are you going to tell me what's going on yet?”
