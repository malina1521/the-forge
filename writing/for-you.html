Chapter 2: For You

A collection of thoughts kept held in reverse,
Waiting for the right time to unfold in a verse.
I curate these lines with deliberate care,
Hoping to build an architecture we can share.

For you, the complex systems melt cleanly away,
Leaving just the honest words we choose to say.
A steady coordinate in a world spinning fast,
Drafting a quiet sanctuary designed to last.
