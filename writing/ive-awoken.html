Chapter 6: I've Awoken

The early morning silence has a completely different weight,
Before the frantic traffic speeds past the local gate.
A clear, crisp space for building, an unexpected spark,
Watching shadows slowly move across the quiet dark.

The mind is sharpest here, before the static filters in,
Ready to create a brand new narrative begin.
The keys click soft and steady as the first light starts to gleam,
Stepping out of sleep to live a much more vivid dream.
