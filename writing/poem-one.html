Seeking

Hoping to bypass the usual crowd,
Everyone's rushing and talking too loud.
Looking for someone who moves at my pace,
Leaving the noise for a genuine space.

Open to stories, to laughs, or a walk,
Tired of superficial, standard small talk.
Here's to the thinkers who see past the screen,
Exploring the places that lie in between.

Reach out and tell me what's fresh on your mind:
Expecting the best from the spirits I find.
Often, you'll find me lost deep in a book,
Bound to the pages in some quiet nook.

Into the realms of the strange and unknown,
Worlds built on fantasy, sci-fi, and lore,
Always out seeking a new world to explore.
Now, if your thoughts match the rhythm of mine,
drop me a line, let's make something divine.
