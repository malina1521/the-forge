M's Morning Routine

Alarm goes off at 5:00 AM.
Snooze until 5:30 AM.
Roll out of bed and head to the kitchen to start the coffee maker.
While coffee brews, feed Artemis and let her outside.
Pour first cup of coffee—black, no sugar.
Sit on the couch for 15 minutes of quiet reflection before checking emails.

6:00 AM – Open the laptop to review overnight customer escalations and check the team dashboard.
6:45 AM – Quick 30-minute power walk around the neighborhood to wake up fully.
7:30 AM – Shower, get dressed, and pour cup number two before diving into the first synchronous operations sync of the day.
