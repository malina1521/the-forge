J's Morning Routine

Wake up without an alarm when the light hits the room.
Spend 10 minutes checking the notes app for any creative dialogue or story hooks that surfaced during sleep.

Grind fresh coffee beans and use the French press.
Drink coffee while looking out the window, completely disconnected from screens for the first hour of the day.
Read a chapter of a book—usually switching between sci-fi world-building references and classic fiction.

Log on to check code repositories and review pipeline automations.
Open the shared anthology workspace to review edits and prep sections for the next collaborative writing block.
