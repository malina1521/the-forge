Chapter 8: Secretly

Guarding a quiet reality beneath the external display,
A collection of draft files hidden completely away.
We check our public masks at the front secure door,
To explore a vulnerability we hadn't mapped before.

Secretly building a world where the phrasing aligns,
Weaving a thread that steadily, quietly entwines.
Let the world trace the metrics and analyze the outer shell,
While we guard the deep spaces where the true stories dwell.
