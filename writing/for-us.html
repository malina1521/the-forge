Chapter 3: For Us

A private sanctuary built out of simple ink,
A place to safely process exactly what we think.
Away from global systems, the corporate metrics scale,
We write a clean alternative, a brand new fluid tale.

This layout belongs completely to the spaces we design,
Where your deliberate stanzas meet up to merge with mine.
No algorithmic pressure, no public eyes to please,
Just two distinct word smiths, writing completely at ease.
